Our Core Volunteer Roles

These are the hands-on roles where your direct involvement makes the biggest difference.

Delivery Volunteer

Be the lifeline for families by delivering essential food packages and supplies directly to their homes. This role is crucial for ensuring aid reaches those who need it most, quickly and efficiently.

Key Details

  • 4-6 hours per week
  • Vehicle required
  • Beirut & surrounding areas

Support Coordinator

Provide vital remote support by being the first point of contact for families seeking help. You'll assess needs, offer guidance, and coordinate with our on-the-ground teams to ensure timely assistance.

Key Details

  • 3-5 hours per week
  • Work from home
  • Arabic & English preferred

Digital Support Volunteer

Leverage your digital skills to amplify our reach and efficiency. Help with social media outreach, website content updates, data entry, or graphic design to support our mission from anywhere.

Key Details

  • 2-4 hours per week
  • Remote work
  • Tech skills helpful

Family Liaison

Build meaningful relationships with the families we serve. Provide ongoing emotional support, check on their well-being, and ensure they feel heard and cared for throughout their journey.

Key Details

  • 5-8 hours per week
  • Strong empathy & communication
  • Field visits required

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering with HAQQ.

What is the time commitment for volunteers?

Time commitments vary by role, but most of our core volunteering positions require 2-8 hours per week. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your availability. Specific details are listed under each volunteer role.

Do I need specific skills or experience?

While some roles benefit from specific skills (e.g., vehicle for delivery, language skills for support), many positions primarily require a compassionate heart and a willingness to help. We provide necessary training for all roles.

How does HAQQ ensure volunteer safety?

Volunteer safety is our top priority. We provide clear guidelines, safety briefings, and ensure all activities are conducted in secure environments. For field roles, volunteers are often paired, and routes are carefully planned.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes, our Digital Support Volunteer and Support Coordinator roles are designed to be performed remotely, allowing you to contribute from anywhere with an internet connection.

How do I apply to become a volunteer?

You can express your interest by clicking the "Join as Volunteer" button below, which will direct you to our contact page. Please send us a WhatsApp message detailing your interest and availability, and our team will get back to you.
