HAQQ: What We Stand For

حركة حَقّ — من الناس، للناس، من أجل لبنان.

What is HAQQ?

HAQQ (حَقّ) is a national Lebanese movement founded to bring dignity, truth, and justice back to our people.

We are not a political party. We are not another failed promise. We are a group of people — young and old — who are tired of waiting for someone else to fix our country.

We believe in action. We believe in truth. And we believe that Lebanon still belongs to its people — not to corruption, not to militias, and not to foreign agendas.

Founded and led by: ألكسندرو غانم

Official launch: 30 حزيران 2025 (June 30, 2025)

Our Core Principles

These values guide every action we take and every decision we make.

Justice (العدالة)

Justice means more than courts — it means equal electricity, clean water, safe schools, and real accountability. We believe everyone deserves to live in dignity, not just survive.

Peace (السلام)

We are tired of hearing bombs while our youth want peace. We don't want to be dragged into foreign wars. If we wanted to fight, we'd all join the army — but we want life, not death.

National Sovereignty (السيادة الوطنية)

We want our entire land back. No excuses. No surrender. Lebanon belongs to its people — not outside forces or occupiers.

Clean Governance (الحكم النظيف)

No more moteur scams. No more stolen public money. Every donation HAQQ receives is used honestly — and publicly documented.

Local Empowerment (دعم الناس فعليًا)

The South and neglected towns are full of people who've been forgotten. We will help them — with or without the government.

The Problems We Solve

HAQQ exists to directly address the most pressing issues facing Lebanon today.

Critical Shortage of Basic Needs

Families across Lebanon face dire scarcity of essential food items, clean drinking water, and life-saving medical supplies.

Failing Educational Infrastructure

Children are deprived of fundamental school supplies, educational materials, and proper learning environments due to systemic neglect.

Systemic Mismanagement & Accountability Crisis

Public resources are diverted or wasted, resulting in deteriorated public services and a profound erosion of trust in governance.

What HAQQ Will Do

Our direct actions to bring tangible change to Lebanese communities.

Food & Aid Distribution

Delivering food boxes to families in need — especially war-affected regions.

School Supply Drives

Providing full kits to students in southern Lebanon who have nothing.

Emergency Relief

Giving basic supplies, shelter support, and hygiene items to displaced families.

Clean-Up Campaigns

Street-level campaigns to restore pride in neglected neighborhoods.

Community Projects

Rebuilding broken areas, one wall, one family, one school at a time.

Establishing Transparent Operations

We implement rigorous financial tracking and public reporting for every donation and expenditure, setting a new standard for honest and accountable aid delivery.
