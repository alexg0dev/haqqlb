Direct Aid & Support

How HAQQ Helps

We provide immediate, direct assistance to Lebanese families in crisis. No bureaucracy, no delays, no hidden agendas - just real help when you need it most.

Get Emergency Help How It Works

Types of Aid We Provide

From emergency food packages to educational support, we cover the essential needs of Lebanese families.

Emergency Food Packages

Complete food boxes with rice, oil, canned goods, bread, and essential groceries delivered directly to your door.

$50 feeds a family for a week

  • Rice, pasta, and grains
  • Canned vegetables and proteins
  • Cooking oil and spices
  • Fresh bread when possible

Direct Cash Aid

Emergency cash assistance for urgent needs like medicine, rent, utilities, or unexpected expenses.

Available through OMT & direct transfer

  • Medical emergencies
  • Rent and utility payments
  • Transportation costs
  • Emergency repairs

School Supplies

Complete school kits for students including notebooks, pens, books, backpacks, and uniforms.

Focus on Southern Lebanon

  • Notebooks and stationery
  • Textbooks and workbooks
  • School bags and uniforms
  • Art and science supplies

Medical Aid

Essential medications, medical supplies, and healthcare support for chronic conditions and emergencies.

Priority for elderly & chronic patients

  • Prescription medications
  • First aid supplies
  • Medical equipment
  • Doctor visit assistance

Emergency Shelter

Temporary housing, blankets, mattresses, and basic furniture for displaced families.

For war-affected families

  • Temporary accommodation
  • Bedding and blankets
  • Basic furniture
  • Heating assistance

Hygiene & Dignity Kits

Personal hygiene items including soap, shampoo, toothpaste, and feminine products.

Essential for displaced families

  • Personal hygiene items
  • Feminine hygiene products
  • Baby care supplies
  • Cleaning supplies

How It Works

Our process is simple, direct, and transparent. No bureaucracy, no delays, no complicated forms.

1

Contact Us

Message us on WhatsApp with your need or situation. Available 24/7 for emergencies.

2

Quick Assessment

We assess your situation and determine the best way to help within hours, not days.

3

Immediate Action

We provide aid within 24-48 hours of contact. Emergency cases get priority.

4

Follow Up

We check in to ensure you're getting the support you need and offer ongoing assistance.

Emergency? Don't Wait!

If you're in immediate need of food, shelter, medical aid, or emergency assistance, contact us right now. We prioritize emergency cases and respond immediately.

1

2

3

Who We Help

HAQQ is here for all Lebanese people in need, regardless of background, religion, or location.

Families in Crisis

Families facing financial hardship, job loss, or unexpected emergencies

Displaced Families

Families displaced by conflict, especially from Southern Lebanon

Students

Children and young adults who need educational support and supplies

Elderly & Vulnerable

Elderly people and those with chronic conditions needing medical aid
